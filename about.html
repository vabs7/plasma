Plasma Engineering Company is a leading manufacturer of LT Control and Power Panels. Established in the year 2005, we are based in Mumbai with our national outreach and serving customers across India.

  5. We have following certificates: ⦁ Icw- 80 kA rms for 1 sec as per IEC 61439 part 1 & 2
    ⦁ 70 kA short circuits rating for 1 sec at CPRI Bhopal
    ⦁ Temperature rise of 80K at CPRI Bhopal
    ⦁ IP65 testing at IDEMI Mumbai
    ⦁ 70 kA short circuits for 1 sec for Bus duct
    ⦁ ISO 9001:2015 ( The Management System)

  6. Our panels are totally type tested (TTA) for its design reliability as per IEC 61439 – 1&2 (Edition 2.0:2011-08). Type Tests include: ⦁ Strength of material and parts
    ⦁ Degree of protection of enclosures
    ⦁ Clearances
    ⦁ Creepage distances
    ⦁ Protection against electric shock
    ⦁ Incorporation of switching device and components
    ⦁ Internal electrical circuits and connections
    ⦁ Terminals for external conductors
    ⦁ Dielectric properties
    ⦁ Temperature-rise limits
    ⦁ Short-circuit withstand strength
    ⦁ Electromagnetic compatibility (EMC)
    ⦁ Mechanical operation

  7. Our Quality Management System is well designed as per ISO 9001 and includes following routine tests as part of overall production. These tests are conducted within Plasma premises. ⦁ Inspection – wiring and electrical operation
    ⦁ Verification of protective measures and continuity of protective circuits
    ⦁ Verification of insulation resistance
    ⦁ HV Test at 2.5kV for 1 min.

  8. We also offer following optional tests as per project requirements, on a chargeable basis: ⦁ Heat Run Test (up to 5000A)
    ⦁ Combined Primary and Secondary Injection Test
    ⦁ Thermography Test at site to assess loose termination.
